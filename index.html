Synopsis

Emberlyn, a young fae from the enchanting world of Elyndor, lived in peace and harmony alongside her friends under the protection of an all powerful King. Beauty filled every corner of Elyndor. It was a land governed by love, light and magic.

Despite the happiness that transcended in Elyndor, a growing shadow loomed in secret, one that would change the fate of Emberlyn's world forever. Like a thief in the night, a dark magic overpowered the land confining its true form.
